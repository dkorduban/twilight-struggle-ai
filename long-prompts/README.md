# long-prompts/

Long-form documents generated by the human author or through conversations with an external LLM (ChatGPT, Claude.ai, etc.) that contain food for thought, architectural plans, or recipes that may need adaptation before use.

## What belongs here

- Architecture deep-dives and design conversations exported from chat
- Q&A threads that explore trade-offs in detail
- Plans or recipes that are directionally correct but require context-specific adaptation
- Analysis documents that informed a decision but are too long/rough to live in `docs/`

## What does NOT belong here

- Final, authoritative decisions → `docs/`
- Project instructions for Claude Code → `CLAUDE.md` at root
- Active specs being implemented → `.claude/plan/`
- Experiment results → `results/`

## Usage

Read these when facing a design decision in the relevant area. They contain reasoning chains and alternative approaches, not just conclusions. Treat them as starting points — the codebase may have evolved since they were written.
